# Playground

[inseri Playground](https://zi.uzh.ch/whp/science-it/inseri/playground/){:target="\_blank"} relies on [WordPress Playground](https://wordpress.github.io/wordpress-playground/){:target="\_blank"} to provide a playground where you can try out our [inseri core](https://wordpress.org/plugins/inseri-core/){:target="\_blank"} plugin and explore (inspect and even edit) all our [showcases](https://zi.uzh.ch/whp/science-it/inseri/showcase/){:target="\_blank"}.

Be aware that your changes in the playground are temporary and will be gone after a page refresh.

## How to use inseri Playground

1. Open [inseri Playground](https://zi.uzh.ch/whp/science-it/inseri/playground/){:target="\_blank"}
2. Click on "Start inseri Playground"
3. Wait until you can see a page "Playground - inseri core"
4. Select a post that you are interested to explore (e.g., "Hello World", available in "Older Posts" blocks)
5. Click Edit Post.
6. Voil√†. You can explore this post!
